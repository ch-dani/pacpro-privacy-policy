Pacpro Scaner Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Sept 01, 2025

PACPRO Scanner respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (“App”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Camera Access: We access your device’s camera solely to allow you to scan barcodes on packages (e.g., FedEx, Amazon, USPS, UPS, and others).
  • Scanned Data: The barcode values we scan are used within the app to identify packages. We do not collect names, photos, or any personal information from the camera feed.
  • App Usage Data: We may collect basic diagnostic data such as crash logs or anonymized performance data to improve the app’s reliability.

2. How We Use Information

  • To perform barcode scanning and display results to you.
  • To process and sync package data to your organization’s servers if configured.
  • To improve app performance and troubleshoot technical issues.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Data collected through scanning is shared only with your connected organization’s systems (if applicable) and is never shared with carriers such as FedEx, Amazon, USPS, or UPS.

4. Data Storage

Scanned barcode data may be stored temporarily on your device or securely transmitted to your organization’s backend system for operational purposes. We do not store this data on our own servers unless your organization’s configuration requires it.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You may contact us to request details about the information we process or to request deletion of any data stored on our systems.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect the data processed by our app. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new effective date on this page.
